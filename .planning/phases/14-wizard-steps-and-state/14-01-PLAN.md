  <action>
Create wizard-types.ts with:
- `WizardStep` type: `'goal' | 'size' | 'features' | 'integrations' | 'design' | 'result'`
- `STEP_ORDER: WizardStep[]` constant array in flow order
- `STEP_LABELS` constant: `['Mal', 'Storrelse', 'Funksjoner', 'Integrasjoner', 'Design', 'Resultat']` (Norwegian)
- `WizardState` interface: `{ currentStep: WizardStep; serviceType: ServiceType | null; sizeId: string | null; featureIds: string[]; integrationIds: string[]; designId: string | null; direction: 1 | -1 }`
- `WizardAction` discriminated union with actions:
  - `SELECT_GOAL` (sets serviceType, resets all downstream, advances to 'size', direction 1)
  - `SELECT_SIZE` (sets sizeId, advances to 'features', direction 1)
  - `TOGGLE_FEATURE` (adds/removes featureId from array, stays on current step)
  - `TOGGLE_INTEGRATION` (adds/removes integrationId from array, stays on current step)
  - `SELECT_DESIGN` (sets designId, advances to 'result', direction 1)
  - `NEXT_STEP` (advances to next step in STEP_ORDER, direction 1)
  - `GO_TO_STEP` (jumps to any step, sets direction based on relative position -- only allow jumping to completed steps i.e. steps before currentStep)
  - `RESET` (returns to initial state, direction -1)

Import `ServiceType` from `@/config/pricing-config`.

Create wizard-reducer.ts with:
- `initialWizardState: WizardState` with goal step, all selections null/empty, direction 1
- `wizardReducer(state, action): WizardState` implementing all actions
- CRITICAL: `SELECT_GOAL` must reset sizeId, featureIds, integrationIds, designId to initial values (Pitfall 1 from research -- different services have different option sets)
- Single-select steps (Goal, Size, Design) auto-advance to next step on selection
- Multi-select steps (Features, Integrations) do NOT auto-advance -- require explicit NEXT_STEP
- `GO_TO_STEP` calculates direction by comparing STEP_ORDER indices
