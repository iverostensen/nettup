**Per D-02:** Awareness variants MUST lead with price anchor hook: "Andre byraer tar 15 000-50 000 kr for en nettside. Vi gjor det for 399 kr/mnd."
**Per D-03:** No em dashes anywhere. Use period or comma.

**Per D-05:** Cover all 3 funnel stages.
