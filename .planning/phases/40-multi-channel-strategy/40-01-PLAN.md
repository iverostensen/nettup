### "## 1. Oversikt" (Executive summary)
- 3-phase rollout summary table: Fase 1 Facebook (mnd 1-3, 70%), Fase 2 Google Ads long-tail (mnd 2-4, 20%), Fase 3 TikTok/ekspansjon (mnd 4+, 10%)
- Total starting budget: 5 000-10 000 kr/mnd
- Goal: 1 klient/maned = lonnsomhet

### "## 2. Fase 1: Facebook og Instagram (Maned 1-3)"
- Role: Primary channel, 70% of budget
- Why first: Lowest CPC in Norway (~8.50 NOK, 24% below global), almost no Norwegian web agency competition on Facebook, visual format suits portfolio showcase
- Budget: 3 500-7 000 kr/mnd (70% of 5 000-10 000)
- Campaign structure: Reference the 3-layer targeting from audience-targeting.md (Kald 70%, Varm 20%, Het 10% of Facebook budget)
- Creative rotation: Reference weekly cadence from Phase 39 (2x scroll-throughs, 1x bold text hook, 1x before/after -- under 2 hrs/week)
- Month 1 focus: A/B test hooks (price anchor vs speed) per testing-plan.md 2x2 matrix
- Month 2: Scale winners, introduce carousel variants
- Month 3: Evaluate for Google Ads activation trigger
- Activation trigger for Fase 2: Facebook CPL stabilized below 700 NOK for 2 consecutive weeks AND minimum 10 leads generated

### "## 3. Fase 2: Google Ads Long-Tail (Maned 2-4)"
- Role: Secondary channel, 20% of budget
- Why: Captures high-intent search traffic that Facebook misses (people actively searching for web agency)
- Why long-tail only: Broad terms ("webbyraa oslo") cost 25-40 NOK CPC with 50+ competitors. Long-tail ("pris pa nettside for liten bedrift", "nettside abonnement") has lower competition and higher intent
- Budget: 1 000-2 000 kr/mnd (20% of total)
- Reference existing Google Ads documentation from v1.6 (campaign structure, RSA variants, setup guide already complete)
- Activation: Can start month 2 if Facebook is running smoothly; does not require Facebook success
- KPIs: CPC < 20 NOK on long-tail terms, CVR > 3% on /nettside-for-bedrift

### "## 4. Fase 3: TikTok og ekspansjon (Maned 4+)"
- Role: Test channel, 10% of budget
- Why: Younger business owners (25-35), growing B2B presence, short-form video content can be repurposed from Facebook creative
- Budget: 500-1 000 kr/mnd (10% of total)
- Activation trigger: Facebook and Google Ads both running profitably (CPL < 700 NOK combined average) for 4 consecutive weeks
- Creative: Repurpose existing 9:16 video content from Facebook (bold text-on-screen, scroll-throughs already in correct aspect ratio)
- Platform-specific: TikTok Ads Manager, similar audience targeting to Facebook cold layer but younger skew (25-40)
- Evaluation period: 4 weeks minimum before kill/scale decision
- NOTE: TikTok is experimental. If CPL > 950 NOK after 4 weeks, reallocate budget to Facebook/Google winners

### "## 6. KPI-mal"
Progressive KPI table (per D-03):

| Periode | KPIer | Begrunnelse |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Maned 1 | CPL (mal: 300-600 NOK) | Umiddelbart malbar, ingen LTV-data enna |
| Maned 2-3 | CPL + ROAS + CTR + CPM + linkklikk | Eksisterende verktoy, ingen ny infrastruktur |
| Maned 4+ | Full CAC/LTV-analyse (utsettes til 10+ betalende kunder med data) | Krever reelle retensjondata for meningsfull LTV |

ROAS for month 2 (per D-04): Use estimated LTV = 399 kr/mnd x estimated retention months. Even rough estimate (e.g., 6 months = 2 394 kr LTV) gives directional ROAS.

### "## 7. Skaleringsregler"
Hybrid approach (per D-05):

**Manedlig budsjett-gulv:** Set a monthly planned spend floor for predictable cash flow. Start at 5 000 kr/mnd; increase floor quarterly based on results.

**CPL-utlost skalering (per D-05):**
- CPL < 500 NOK: Ok budsjett med 20% hvert 3. dag (per Phase 39 D-18). Maks 50% okning per uke.
- CPL 500-950 NOK: Hold budsjett stabilt. Optimaliser kreativ og malgrupper.
- CPL > 950 NOK: Pause kampanjen. Evaluer kreativ, malgruppe, og landingsside.
- Frekvens > 3: Roter kreativ (ny video/bilde), behold hook og malgruppe.
- CTR < 0.5%: Stopp annonsesettet. Test ny hook eller visuell.

**Kanalskalering:**
- Fase 1 > 2 trigger: Facebook CPL < 700 NOK i 2 sammenhengende uker + minimum 10 leads
- Fase 2 > 3 trigger: Facebook + Google Ads begge lonsomme (CPL < 700 NOK snitt) i 4 uker
- Budsjett-reallokering: Flytt 10% fra underperformende kanal til vinner manedlig

- [ ] Sjekk frekvens per annonsesett
- [ ] Roter kreativ hvis frekvens > 2.5
- [ ] Vurder budsjett-okning hvis CPL < 500 NOK i 3+ dager

### "## 8. Break-even-analyse"
Break-even math (STRAT-02 requirement):

**Forutsetninger:**
- Abonnement: 399 kr/mnd
- Oppstartskostnad: 0 kr
- Manedlig annonsebudsjett: 5 000 kr
- Estimert CPL: 500 NOK (realistisk midtpunkt)
- Leads per maned: ~10 (5 000 / 500)
- Lead-til-klient-konverteringsrate: ~10% (1 av 10 leads blir klient)
- Klienter per maned: ~1

**Lonnsomhetsberegning:**
- Manedlig inntekt per klient: 399 kr
- Estimert retensjon: 12+ maneder (abonnementsmodell)
- LTV per klient: 399 x 12 = 4 788 kr
- CAC (kostnad per klient): 5 000 kr (hele manedsbudsjettet / 1 klient)
- LTV:CAC ratio: 4 788 / 5 000 = 0.96 (break-even etter 12.5 maneder)

**Sensitivitetsanalyse (table):**

| Scenario | CPL | Leads/mnd | Klienter/mnd | CAC | LTV (12 mnd) | LTV:CAC |
|----------|-----|-----------|--------------|-----|-------------|---------|
| Pessimistisk | 950 NOK | 5 | 0.5 | 10 000 kr | 4 788 kr | 0.48 |
| Realistisk | 500 NOK | 10 | 1 | 5 000 kr | 4 788 kr | 0.96 |
| Optimistisk | 300 NOK | 17 | 1.7 | 2 941 kr | 4 788 kr | 1.63 |

**Konklusjon:** Kampanjen er lonnsam ved 1 ny klient per maned, gitt retensjon over 12 maneder. Med 2+ klienter per maned (optimistisk scenario) er ROI positiv fra maned 7. Merverdi utover abonnement (tilleggstjenester, oppgraderinger) forbedrer LTV ytterligere.
