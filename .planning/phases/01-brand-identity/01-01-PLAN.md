Write a brand document in Norwegian (bokmål) with these sections:

**# Nettup Brand**

**## Oppdraget (The Mission)**
Articulate "vi revolusjonerer webbyråbransjen" — but make it concrete. What does revolutionizing mean for Nettup? Speed + honesty + modern craft vs. the industry norm of slow agencies, opaque pricing, outdated tech. Keep it one strong paragraph, not bullet points.

**## Hvem vi er for (Who We're For)**
Primary: Small local businesses — cafés, tradespeople, clinics — who need a professional online presence but have been burned by generic agencies charging too much and delivering too little.
Secondary readiness: Growth-stage SMBs. The brand should feel capable of this step even if it isn't the lead audience yet.
One concrete sentence on what we are NOT for (e.g., we are not for enterprise, not for clients who want committees and 6-month timelines).

**## Tone of Voice**
Rules for how Nettup writes. Three rules, each with:
- The rule (imperative form)
- An on-brand example sentence (Norwegian)
- An off-brand example sentence (Norwegian)

Derive from these locked values: Direct but warm. Punchy headlines. Friendly, plain-language explanations. No corporate speak.

Example rule format:
> **Lei med fordelene, ikke funksjonene** — Skriv "Du får kunder som finner deg på Google" ikke "Vi implementerer SEO-optimalisering".

**## Hva vi sier / Hva vi ikke sier (Contrast Table)**
A markdown table with two columns: "Sier vi" | "Sier vi ikke"
Minimum 8 rows. Derive from the brand values. Examples to include:
- "Nettside klar på 2 uker" vs. "Leveringstid avhenger av prosjektets kompleksitet"
- "Fast pris, ingen overraskelser" vs. "Pris oppgis etter innledende konsultasjon"
- "Vi er ekte folk, ikke et stort byrå" vs. "Vi er et ledende digitalt transformasjonsbyrå"

**## Visuelle verdier (Visual Values)**
Three bullet points capturing what the site should feel like visually. Reference Framer.com and Resend.com. Not a list of colors — a list of feelings/impressions. Example:
- Mørkt og trygt — ikke kaldt. Bakgrunnen gir ro, fargemomentene gir energi.
- Typografien gjør jobben — overskrifter er statements, ikke dekorasjon.
- Premium, ikke pralende — hvert designvalg er intentional, ingenting er tilfeldig.

**## Kopiprinsipper (Copy Principles)**
Maximum 3 principles with examples. Derive from the brand. Examples:
1. Konkret over vag — "2 uker" ikke "raskt"
2. Aktiv stemme — "Vi bygger" ikke "Det bygges"
3. Kundens utbytte først — lead with what they get, not what we do

**Tone:** The document itself should demonstrate the brand voice — write it as Nettup would write, not as a brand consultant would.
