How gradient text works:
- `bg-gradient-to-r from-brand to-text` — linear gradient left-to-right, cyan (#06b6d4) to white (#F8FAFC)
- `bg-clip-text` — clips the background to the text shape (generates both `background-clip: text` and `-webkit-background-clip: text`)
- `text-transparent` — sets `color: transparent` so the gradient shows through

`from-brand` and `to-text` are existing Tailwind color tokens (from tailwind.config.ts colors.brand.DEFAULT and colors.text.DEFAULT).

No other changes to RotatingText.tsx — do not touch the animation logic, timing, or word list.
